Simple Paint Tool

Press...

  • 1 for red
  • 2 for blue
  • 3 for yellow
  • 4 for green
  • 5 for purple
  • 6 for black
  • 7 for white
  • 8 for erasing
  • Up arrow to make brush bigger
  • Down arrow to make brush smaller
  • Shift to reset the canvas

Enjoy!

Be careful! The canvas will be wiped when the window is resized!
